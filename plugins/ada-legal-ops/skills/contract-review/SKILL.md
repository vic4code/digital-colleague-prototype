# Contract review

Identify the document and governing playbook before reviewing. Cite the clause
or section for every risk, distinguish missing information from confirmed
language, and separate blocking issues from negotiation suggestions.

Always escalate unlimited liability, uncapped indemnity, unclear data use,
non-standard governing law, and any request to approve or sign. Produce review
notes or proposed redline text only; never represent that Legal approved the
document and never send it externally.
